This project is part of my contribution to the PKM-RE (Student Creativity Program for Exact Research) team, which focuses on developing social media content for the Instagram platform. In this project, I played an important role in creating various visual assets using Figma. I strived to come up with creative and informative designs to increase engagement and interaction on social media. This project not only gave me valuable experience in graphic design and social media strategy, but also strengthened my ability to collaborate and innovate in a team.
